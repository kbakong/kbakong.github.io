About me

Hello! I am Killian Bakong, a PhD student in computer science in the Argo team at Inria Paris. I am currently working under the supervision of Kevin Scaman and Laurent Massoulié.

Prior to my PhD, I obtained the master's degree MVA (Mathématiques, Vision, Apprentissage) from ENS Paris-Saclay, as well as an engineering degree from École Nationale des Ponts et Chaussées.

Research

My research focuses on optimization and deep learning theory, particularly in distributed settings. I study how the decentralization of computing nodes affects learning from both theoretical and empirical perspectives. My goal is to leverage the characteristics of such distributed setups to train models more efficiently.
